What Each Tool Is For

The Previty Method™ operates across three tiers — individual, organizational, and community. Each tier answers a different question for a different audience. Understanding which tool to use for your purpose is important.

Tier 2 · Population Risk Engine
  • Designed for health plan medical directors, care management teams, and population health administrators
  • Input: a manually-entered cohort of known patients with clinical data (age, diagnoses, ED visits, admissions, clinical complexity)
  • Output: risk tier classification (High / Moderate / Low) per patient, intervention pathway assignments, and cohort-level distribution
  • Use case: care management prioritization, complex case identification, care gap closure planning, 30/60/90-day outreach protocols
  • Aligned to NCQA Complex Case Management and CMS HCC frameworks
  • Not intended for: epidemiological research, geographic analysis, or understanding a community's aggregate health burden
Community Health Intelligence
  • Designed for health plans entering new markets, ACOs analyzing service areas, public health programs, and AI health companies evaluating deployment populations
  • Input: a US county or California ZIP code, with optional demographic filters (race/ethnicity, age group, income)
  • Output: population-level chronic disease burden, lifestyle medicine pillar scores, SDOH indicators, Community Health Age™, and an NCQA-aligned intervention priority matrix
  • Data sources: CDC PLACES 2023 (all US counties), CDPH County Health Status Profiles (all 58 California counties), CDPH CHIS (demographic stratification)
  • Not intended for: individual patient care decisions or clinical diagnosis

Tier 1 · Individual Health Assessment

  • Designed for individuals seeking to understand their personal chronic disease risk and lifestyle medicine gaps
  • Input: self-reported biometrics, lab values, lifestyle behaviors, and health history — all entered and computed locally in the browser
  • Output: Health Age vs. biological age, 10-disease risk profile, 6-pillar lifestyle scorecard, personalized action plan, and life expectancy estimate
  • For health plans and employers: can be deployed as a member/employee wellness tool to generate population-level engagement and identify high-risk individuals for outreach
  • No data is collected or stored. All calculations run in your browser. HIPAA not applicable (no data transmission).
